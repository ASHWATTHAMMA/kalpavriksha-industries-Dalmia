About Us

At Kalpavriksha Pvt Ltd, we believe farming is more than a livelihood—it's the heartbeat of our nation. Founded on 3rd January 2025, Kalpvriksha was created to bridge the gap between traditional agriculture and cutting-edge technology, helping farmers make confident, data-driven decisions for a greener tomorrow.

The name Kalpavriksha originates from the Sanskrit word “Kalpavṛkṣa”, meaning a wish-fulfilling divine tree. In ancient Indian mythology, it symbolizes a source of abundance, prosperity, and fulfillment, granting every sincere wish—reflecting the idea of limitless potential and benevolent creation.

Our Vision

To empower every farmer with tools that think, learn, and adapt—making farming effortless, sustainable, and rewarding. We dream of a future where every seed is planted with knowledge, every drop of water counts, and every harvest tells a story of success.

Kalpvriksha isn't just building machines; we're building trust and empowerment in the hands of farmers who form India's backbone.

Our Journey

It began with a simple soil test and a big realization—farmers face uncertainty every single day. How healthy is the soil? When should irrigation start? What if pests strike overnight? Kalpvriksha was born to answer these questions using AI, IoT, and data analytics—all while keeping things simple.

From local farms to national innovation expos, Kalpvriksha has grown from a small idea into a powerful movement that's redefining agriculture with intelligence and care.

Innovation Rooted in Purpose

Every technology we design aims to make a real difference—from monitoring soil health to automating irrigation and protecting crops. Our AI-driven solutions are tested in real farms with real farmers, ensuring reliability and simplicity.
